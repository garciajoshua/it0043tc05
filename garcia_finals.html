ABOUT ME

Hey there! I'm John Joshua Garcia, and I'm studying for a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology with a focus on Cybersecurity at FEU Institute of Technology. Cybersecurity gets me fired up when it comes to ethical hacking and digital forensics. I grew up in San Pablo City, Laguna. and I've always had a thing when it comes to Technology and if I'm not diving into code, you'll find me playing games or shooting hoops - both feed my competitive side and keep my mind sharp.

My fascination with cybersecurity began early. As a child, I spent countless hours troubleshooting computer problems, guided by YouTube tutorials. My family quickly recognized my tech aptitude, which ignited my dream of a career in Information Technology.

Now, I'm thrilled to study this field and can't wait to put my skills to use in protecting companies from online threats. Picking cybersecurity as my major was a clear choice. I like the tests and benefits this field offers. The program improves my ability to think and builds up my tech skills. Writing code in C++, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript is tough but very satisfying. This shows that when you love something, getting past hurdles turns into a fun adventure.
